Our Policies & Service Agreement

By scheduling a service with EverClean Housekeeping, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

1. Our 24-Hour Satisfaction Guarantee

How to Report an Issue: If you are not satisfied with any aspect of our recent service, please notify us within 24 hours of the cleaning by email or phone, including photos of the areas in question.

Our Promise for Significant Issues: For significant issues, such as an entire room or a major checklist category being missed (e.g., an entire bathroom was not cleaned, floors were not mopped), we will send a cleaner back to your home at our earliest convenience to re-clean the specific areas, at no additional cost.

Our Promise for Minor Issues: For minor, isolated issues (e.g., a single missed window sill, one dusty vent), a re-clean visit will not be scheduled. Instead, we will note your account and apply a credit for the missed item to your next scheduled cleaning service.

Exclusions: This guarantee does not include a full or partial refund of your service fee. Our commitment is to correct any issues and ensure the job is done to your satisfaction. Complaints filed more than 24 hours after the service is complete will be addressed at our discretion.

2. Preparing for Your Cleaning

Tidying: Please pick up any clothing, toys, or other personal items from the floor. Our teams will clean around cluttered areas. If you require assistance with tidying, please book our "Tidying & Organizing" add-on service.

Dishes: Washing dishes is not part of our cleaning packages. Please ensure your sink is clear so we can properly clean and sanitize it.

Valuables: Please secure any valuables, heirlooms, or irreplaceable items.

3. Service Scope Clarifications

Deep Cleaning Appliances: Our Deep Cleaning service includes the interior and exterior cleaning of one standard-sized refrigerator and one standard-sized oven located within the main kitchen. This does not include additional appliances such as a second refrigerator/freezer in a garage, basement, or other area. Cleaning of additional appliances can be scheduled for a separate fee.

Post-Construction Cleaning: This service is for the removal of construction dust and debris from newly installed surfaces. It does not include the removal of large items, construction materials, or trash hauling.

Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning: This service is designed for empty homes and includes cleaning the inside of empty cabinets, drawers, and closets. It does not include cleaning the interior of furnished cabinets or closets.

4. Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

We reserve a specific time slot for your home. We require a minimum of 48 hours' notice for any cancellations or rescheduling of your appointment.

Cancellations made with less than 48 hours' notice are subject to a cancellation fee of $50.

If our cleaners are unable to access the property upon arrival (e.g., locked out), a lockout fee equivalent to 50% of the scheduled base cleaning cost (excluding add-ons) will be charged.

5. Pricing & Payment

Card on File: To secure your booking, we require a valid credit or debit card to be kept on file in our secure payment system. Your card will not be charged until after your service is complete.

Pricing Adjustments: All initial prices are estimates based on the information provided. We reserve the right to adjust the final price upon an initial walkthrough if the home's condition or size is significantly different than described. Any changes will be communicated and agreed upon before work begins.

Payment Due: Payment is due in full upon completion of the service. An invoice will be sent to your email on file, which can be paid securely online.

Late Payments: If the invoice is not paid within 72 hours, we will automatically charge the card on file. If the payment is declined and the invoice remains unpaid after 7 days, a late fee of $25 will be added.

6. Breakage & Damage Policy

Our teams are trained to be exceptionally careful. However, accidents can happen. EverClean Housekeeping is insured and bonded.

Any breakage or damage must be reported to our office within 24 hours of the service.

We will make every effort to repair or replace the damaged item. If an item cannot be repaired or replaced, our insurance provider will handle the claim.

7. Safety & Right to Refuse Service

The safety of our cleaners is paramount. We reserve the right to refuse or terminate service for any reason, including but not limited to: unsafe conditions (e.g., biohazards, pests, aggressive pets), presence of firearms that are not secured, or disrespectful or inappropriate behavior from anyone on the property.
